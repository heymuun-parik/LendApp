# Lend and Earn App
Lend and Earn Money
_Ever thought that you can earn money on the stuff that you have? Just by lending?_

# Categories
- Tough Job gear: High quality expensive tools that take up a lot of space. Examples include lawnmowers, car jack stands, power tools
- Hobyist and Pro-Sumer Gear: These target the hobyists trying to experiment before buying. Examples include DSLR Cameras, guitars, drum kits, outdoor and adventure tools like bike racks, premium coolers.
- Niche Mobility & Hauling: Pickup trucks, utility trailers
- Custom: Build a community in your locality or college, and earn by lending other items such as books and other resources.
